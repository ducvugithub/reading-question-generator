Microsoft was founded in 1975 by Bill Gates and Paul Allen. The company developed software products and established itself as a major technology leader. Steve Ballmer joined Microsoft in 1980 and became the CEO in 2000. Under his leadership, Microsoft expanded its business to cloud computing and other domains. Satya Nadella took over as CEO in 2014 and introduced new strategic directions. Apple was founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne. The company created innovative personal computers and later developed the iPhone. Tim Cook became CEO in 2011 and led Apple through significant growth. Amazon was founded in 1994 by Jeff Bezos as an online bookstore. It expanded into e-commerce and cloud computing services. Andy Jassy became CEO in 2021 and continued the company's expansion into various sectors.
